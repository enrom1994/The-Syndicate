# üî´ **IV. PvP (Attacks & Rivals) Economy**

PvP is **Stamina-driven** (NOT Energy).
Regenerates slower = encourages purchases.

---

## **Stamina System**

* Max Stamina: 50
* Regen: 1 every 4 minutes ‚Üí 200 minutes to refill

Players get **~12 attacks every 2‚Äì3 hours** without spending.

---

## **Attack Mechanics**

Attacking another player yields:

* Cash (small %)
* Respect (PvP ranking)
* Chance to steal items
* Chance to injure the opponent

---

## **PvP Loot Formula**

**Cash stolen = 5% of victim‚Äôs un-bankrolled cash**

* Max steal cap = your character strength √ó 100
* Encourages players to BANK cash (sink mechanic)

**Respect earned = difference in level bracket**

* +10 for equal level
* +15 for higher level
* +5 for lower level
* +25 for streaks
